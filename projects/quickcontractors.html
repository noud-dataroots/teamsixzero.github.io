Quick Task App

We redesigned the Quick Task app to give contractors get the best possible experience. We made global search persistent on all screens, we focused on showing ‘New Jobs’ up front so contractors see them as soon as possible, and we simplified the navigation to make getting around easier than ever. Try the prototype

My Jobs

Previously known as the ‘Dashboard’, the ‘My Jobs’ redesign gives users the ability to see what’s happening with their jobs faster than ever. Less clicks and more information upfront. Try the prototype

Single Job

The redesigned Job screen makes understanding the status of a job easier than ever. We broke down the information into multiple tabs and created the ‘Overview’ screen, which gives you a quick snapshot of all the most important information in one place. Try the prototype

Website

Our redesign reduces the number of items in the header to focus users on the most important information they need in order to become a customer.

How It Works

We simplified the ‘How it works’ section so new visitors can understand the process faster.
