Thinkific enables entrepreneurs to create, market, sell, and deliver their own online courses. Here are a few ways we helped them in their mission to revolutionize the way people learn and earn online. Visit Thinkific

User Groups

We designed User Groups to give schools a better way to organize existing students and sell to new ones.

{% include section-expertise.html title="Expertise" expertise="Research, UX, UI Design" %}

Main Menu

We redesigned the Thinkific main navigation to be friendlier, more powerful for everyone, and include global search. With such a diverse user base we spent a lot of time understanding what people different disliked about the current navigation, how they navigated the app, and what features they used the most in order to understand how we could improve their experience and productivity.

Course Builder

Thousands of people use the builder every day to build and manage their online courses.

The drag and drop course builder interface allows people of all technical abilities to create their own online course without having to hire a team of developers.
